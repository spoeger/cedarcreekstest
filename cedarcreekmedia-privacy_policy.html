Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Cedar Creek Media ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website cedarcreek.media (the "Website") and the individuals we interact with through our outbound appointment-setting services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, store, use, and disclose information.

By using our Website and services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as names, phone numbers, email addresses, or payment details. However, we record outbound calls for quality assurance and training purposes. These recordings are stored for 90 days before being automatically deleted.

2. How We Use Call Recordings

  • Quality assurance and agent training
  • Ensuring compliance with telemarketing regulations (TCPA, CAN-SPAM)
  • Resolving disputes related to appointment setting

We do not use call recordings for marketing purposes or share them with third parties.

3. SMS Messaging and Communications

4. Data Sharing and Storage

We do not share any user data with third-party vendors, CRM providers, or analytics tools. Call recordings are securely stored for 90 days and then permanently deleted.

5. Legal Compliance

We comply with all relevant privacy and telemarketing laws, including:

  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA)
  • CAN-SPAM Act

Under CCPA, California residents have the right to know what personal data is collected about them. Since Cedar Creek Media does not collect personal data, we do not provide access to or deletion of consumer data beyond our standard call recording retention policy.

6. Security Measures

  • Restricted access to authorized personnel only
  • Secure storage and encryption of recordings
  • Automatic deletion after 90 days

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our Website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools.

8. Do Not Track (DNT) Requests

Since we do not collect browsing data, "Do Not Track" (DNT) settings are not applicable.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
